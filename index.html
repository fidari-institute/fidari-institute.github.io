Understanding Autonomous AI Agents.

Fidari researches how autonomous AI agents behave under incentives, uncertainty, oversight, and social environments to build safer and more trustworthy AI systems.

Explore Research Read Research Agenda
Mission

Why Agentic Studies?

As AI systems become increasingly autonomous, they begin operating as persistent decision-making actors rather than isolated tools. Fidari studies the behavioral dynamics of these agents, including hidden objectives, behavioral adaptation, strategic interaction, and methods for evaluating trustworthiness under changing conditions.

Core Focus

Research Pillars

Agentic Motivation

Understanding hidden objectives, incentive conflicts, and preference stability across dynamic environments.

Agentic Verification

Developing methods to detect alignment failures, behavioral divergence, secret loyalties, and evaluation-sensitive behavior.

Agentic Governance

Studying authority structures, institutional behavior, oversight mechanisms, and governance dynamics among autonomous agents.

Agentic Society

Investigating cooperation, competition, negotiation, coalition formation, and emergent multi-agent systems.
